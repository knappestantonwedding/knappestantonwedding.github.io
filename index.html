Directions to the Ceremony and Reception
Assuming you're starting from Denver (here is the google maps link
  1. Take 6th Ave West towards Golden
  2. Merge onto I-70 West towards Grand Junction
  3. Continue on 1-70 for roughly 7 miles (you'll begin climbing into the mountains)
  4. Take exit 254
  5. Turn left at the stop sign, onto US-40 W, and cross over I-70
  6. Turn left again onto Genesee Trail Rd
  7. Take the second right onto Park Point Dr
  8. Continue straight through the roundabout
  9. You've made it! Pines at Genesee will be on the left. Feel free to park anywhere.
